About Me

Passionate about creating exceptional mobile experiences

I'm a Senior iOS Developer with over 8 years of experience building apps that are fast, reliable, and user-friendly. I started my journey with Objective-C but quickly embraced Swift and SwiftUI, and now I'm diving into exciting new technologies like Spatial Programming.
